Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

How do I search for books in the system?

Use the search bar located on the home page to search for books by title, author, or ISBN. You can also filter results by genre or publication year.

How do I renew borrowed books?

Go to your account page and click on the "My Borrowed Books" section. From there, you can select the book you want to renew and choose the new return date.
